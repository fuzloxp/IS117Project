Modifications

Modifications or mods are extra additions that builders can make to the keyboard that can improve or change aspects of a keyboard. Mods can alter any aspect of a board. From the sound and look, to the feel and function. Foam can be added into the case to take away from the hallow sound of a board. Cut up bandaids can be put into switches to make for a softer click. Lubricant can be put in the switches, stabilizers, and springs to improve smoothness. As time goes forward people only come up with more and more modifications for boards.
