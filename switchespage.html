Switches/Stabs

Switches are the individual mechanism that is triggered for each button press. They contribute the most towards the sound of a keyboard. There are many different kinds of switches. Some of them are smoother and quite while others are harder and clickier. The number of different kinds of switches are only limited by the people who create them. Different switches can be taken apart and combined with other switches. Switches work in tandem with stabilizers as well. Longer keys such as the space bar and shift keys need stabilizers that ensure a firm key press regardless of where the user pushes down on the key.
