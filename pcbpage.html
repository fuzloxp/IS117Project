PCB/Mounting

A PCB is the nervous system of a keyboard. It is a circuit board that lays under the keycaps and case and lets each keystroke be registered on the device you are using it on. The format of the PCB also accommodates for the size of the keyboard. The PCB can also house certain cosmetics such as lighting. Another major aspect is the mounting of the keyboard. The mounting style of a keyboard determines how a keyboard will feel and also look. Some mounting styles use screws and nuts, while others use rubber bands to make for a softer and less rattle filled typing sound.
